FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

  • What types of investors do you work with?
    +
    We work with family offices, institutional investors, HNW individuals, and corporate capital — primarily from Hong Kong, China, and the broader Asia-Pacific region.
  • What is the minimum investment size?
    +
    Investment minimums vary by opportunity and structure. Please contact us directly to discuss your investment appetite and we'll match you with appropriate opportunities.
  • Do you work with Australian developers?
    +
    Yes — A.P.Partnerships works directly with Australian developers, landowners, and operators. If you have a project seeking capital, structuring, or a co-investment partner, we'd like to hear from you.
  • How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
    +
    We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, please contact us directly via email or phone.
  • How do I access the A.P.P Fund?
    +
    The A.P.P Fund is available to qualified investors via our Hong Kong structure. Please get in touch with your investment objectives and we'll provide full documentation and onboarding details.
