Market Overview

Vietnam's Structural
Investment Moment

Vietnam is undergoing one of the most significant economic transitions in Asia — driven by manufacturing relocation from China, rapid urbanisation, and a government committed to major infrastructure investment. The combination of young demographics, rising incomes, and structural supply gaps is creating a compelling window for real asset capital.

A.P.Property is developing its Vietnam presence with a focus on infrastructure, industrial, and logistics opportunities — where Asian capital, including from our Hong Kong and Chinese networks, can be deployed into assets with strong fundamental demand.

Our View

Why This Market.
Why Now.

The structural drivers and timing factors that make this market compelling for cross-border capital.

01

Manufacturing Relocation

Vietnam is the primary beneficiary of supply chain diversification from China — with over USD 30B in committed FDI annually supporting industrial demand.

02

Government Infrastructure Commitment

Vietnam's National Infrastructure Master Plan targets VND 50+ trillion in annual infrastructure spend through 2030, creating durable demand for real asset capital.

03

Demographic Dividend

A population of over 100 million with a median age under 32 and rapidly rising incomes is creating sustained demand for housing, logistics, and urban infrastructure.

04

Early Positioning Advantage

Institutional-grade investment structures in Vietnam are still developing — allowing well-connected capital to access assets at attractive entry points before competition intensifies.

05

Regional Capital Gateway

Vietnam sits at the intersection of Chinese manufacturing capital outflow and Southeast Asian consumption growth — making it a natural focus for cross-regional capital deployment.
