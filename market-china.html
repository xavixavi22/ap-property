Market Overview

China as a
Primary Capital Source

Chinese capital — from family offices, high-net-worth individuals, and institutional networks across tier 1 and tier 2 cities — represents one of the most significant pools of investable capital seeking offshore real asset exposure. Australia is consistently among the top preferred destinations.

A.P.Property's networks span Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Chengdu, and beyond. We facilitate introductions, structure participation, and manage the compliance and governance requirements that allow Chinese capital to access Australian opportunities correctly.

Our View

Why This Market.
Why Now.

The structural drivers and timing factors that make this market compelling for cross-border capital.

01

Offshore Diversification

Chinese institutional and HNWI capital is actively seeking diversification into stable, transparent, common law jurisdictions — Australia is consistently first choice.

02

Yield Premium

Australian real assets offer yield premiums of 150–300bps above comparable Chinese domestic assets, with superior legal protection and exit liquidity.

03

Education and Migration Link

Strong migration and education links between China and Australia create natural alignment between capital origin and asset location.

04

Established Relationships

A.P.Property's networks have been built over years of operating in both markets — providing genuine introductory access, not cold outreach.

05

Structured Access

Through the HK LPF and co-investment structures, Chinese capital can access Australian real assets with institutional governance, tax efficiency, and full compliance.
